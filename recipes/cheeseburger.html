Cheeseburger

Description

A classic cheeseburger.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Hamburger meat
  • Bun
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Cook the burger for a few minutes on one side
  2. flip the burger. After a couple minutes, put the cheese on.
  3. Toast your bun.
  4. Get your toppings and condiments ready on your bun.
  5. Put your burger together.
